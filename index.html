Frequently Asked Questions

How does HEX generate returns?

HEX's smart contract sets an annual inflation rate of approximately 3.69%. Since not all holders stake, all inflation rewards are distributed among the stakers, resulting in a high APY.

What happens if I withdraw my funds early?

HEX rewards honest behavior. If you exit your stake before the agreed-upon term, the contract will deduct a portion of your principal and all interest as a penalty.
